Account and Data Deletion – THE CUT Station

This page explains how users of THE CUT Station can request the deletion of their account and all associated personal data.

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly within the THE CUT Station app:

  • Open the THE CUT Station app
  • Go to Account → Account Settings → Delete Account
  • Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile information
  • Authentication and login data
  • Appointment and check-in / check-out records

Data retention

Certain completed transaction or service records may be retained for up to 30 days to comply with legal or operational requirements.
