Delete Your Account and Data

Users of the [Your App Name] app can delete their account and all associated data directly within the app.

How to delete your account:

  1. Open the app and go to Account → Account Settings → Delete Account.
  2. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your account.

Data deleted:

  • User profile and authentication data
  • Appointment and check-in/out records

Data retained:

Completed transactions or service records may be retained for up to 30 days for operational or legal purposes.
